I’m Martin McDowell DrVetMed, a senior experienced small animal veterinary surgeon, former Clinical Director, offering vet locum services round the UK but preferably in Herefordshire, Gloucestershire, Worcestershire, Shropshire and south/mid Wales.

If you would require my locum services or would like more information, please get in touch with me through the contact form or directly via my email me@martin.vet. My CV and references are available on request.

Experience

I have 2005 years of experience in practising small animal veterinary medicine both in the UK and abroad. I worked for 5 years in a small animal practice in Wales until 2012, after which I started locuming full-time across the UK until December 2019 when I was offered a position as a Clinical Director in Herefordshire just in time for the COVID-19 pandemic to strike.

Although I have a photo of a lovely tawny owl on this page I do not usually see exotics.
